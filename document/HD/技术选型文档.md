*   **核心框架与语言:** Python, Flask
*   **数据库与 ORM:** MySQL, SQLAlchemy, Alembic
*   **认证与授权:** JWT (Flask-JWT-Extended)
*   **API 与序列化:** Flask-RESTX, Marshmallow
*   **缓存:** Redis (Flask-Caching)
*   **异步任务与消息队列:** Celery, Redis
*   **实时通信:** WebSocket (Flask-SocketIO)
*   **搜索:** Elasticsearch
*   **部署:** Gunicorn / uWSGI
